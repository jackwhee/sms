CSO暑校学生报名须知

1. 学生需要根据学费付款方式缴纳申请费2184 元（420 CAD）或全额学费，并将付款凭证提交，CSO暑期学校收到并确认付款信息后，小助手将在 1-2个工作日添加微信，核对基本信息，并对接专属课程顾问进行课程咨询。


2. 学生需要确保提供的微信号、邮箱真实有效，用于及时接收CSO暑校相关通知（如需更换联系方式请于开课前及时沟通CSO暑校小助手。）


3. 学生应该积极配合CSO暑校小助手，便于沟通，收取材料，确保审课/上课/转学分的顺利进行。


4. 申请费2184 RMB（420 CAD）为不可退款费用（NON-REFUNDABLE）；CSO暑校学生统一以最终缴纳总学费（申请费和学费）时间来计算优惠费用


5. 审课/转学分个人问题不给予退费，例如：在校成绩不达标；在校费用未交齐等


6. 暑校所有相关问题以CSO暑校官网信息为准，CSO暑校官网： www.canadasummer.ca


7. CSO暑校保有最终解释权


CSO Summer School Registration Instructions

1. Students are required to pay an application fee of RMB 2,184 (420 CAD) or full tuition fee according to the tuition payment method and submit the payment voucher. After the CSO Summer School receives and confirms the payment information, the CSO assistant will add students in 1-2 business days on WeChat, check basic information, and consult with the exclusive course consultants.


2. Students need to ensure that the WeChat account number and id and Email address provided are true and valid and are used to receive timely notifications from CSO Summer School. (If you need to change the contact information, please communicate with CSO Summer School Assistant in advance before the start of the course.)


3. Students should actively cooperate with the CSO Summer School Assistant to facilitate communication and collect materials to ensure the smooth progress of the lecture/class/transfer credit.


4. The application fee of 2184 RMB (420 CAD) is non-refundable (NON-REFUNDABLE); CSO Summer School students will calculate the preferential fee by paying the total tuition fee (application fee and tuition fee).


5. No refunds will be given for individual issues in the course/transfer credits, for example: there is not a good academic standard; there is an outstanding debts on your University account.


6. All relevant issues of the summer school are subject to the information of CSO Summer School website. CSO Summer School official website: www.canadasummer.ca


7. CSO Summer School reserves the right of final interpretation
